Process

How an escrow transaction moves.

01

Agree & create

Buyer and seller agree on terms; KudiEscrow opens a transaction with a unique reference ID and a private chat.

02

Fund escrow

Buyer deposits payment. Funds are held securely — the seller is notified but cannot access them yet.

03

Ship & track

Seller fulfils the order. Logistics partners update pickup, transit, and customs status live on the tracking page.

04

Confirm delivery

Buyer confirms the goods match the agreement — or opens a dispute if something's wrong.

05

Release funds

Once confirmed, escrow releases payment to the seller instantly. Every step is logged to an audit trail.
